## Service description

DayQuest creates location-based scavenger hunts from place and source data. Generated clues, place facts, routes, distances, and availability can be wrong, incomplete, or outdated. DayQuest is not an emergency, navigation, traffic, accessibility, or professional safety service.

## Real-world safety

Use judgment. Stay aware of people, traffic, weather, terrain, opening hours, and device battery. Obey laws, property boundaries, posted rules, and instructions from authorities or property owners. Do not trespass. Skip or reveal any stop that is closed, inaccessible, inappropriate, or feels unsafe. Do not use the app while driving or in any way that distracts you from moving safely.

## Accounts and guest use

An account is optional. Users are responsible for activity through their sign-in credentials. Account deletion is available in Profile. Guest reset is a separate on-device action. ## Photos, sharing, friends, and feedback

Quest photos remain local in the represented app unless the user chooses the system share action. Users must have the right to capture and share an image and should respect privacy and venue rules. Friend invites expose the account's user ID in a DayQuest deep link. Display names and avatars are visible to relevant social/leaderboard users. Do not submit unlawful, abusive, infringing, deceptive, or privacy-invasive feedback or shared content.
