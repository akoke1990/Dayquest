## Quick help

- **Location:** DayQuest requests foreground location when starting/planning a quest. Stop or leave the active quest to stop route collection by the app.
- **Camera/photos:** Camera use is optional; use the skip/collect path. Quest photos are local in the represented build.
- **Analytics choice:** Settings → Privacy & legal.
- **Reset guest data:** Settings → Privacy & data → Reset guest data.
- **Delete a signed-in account:** Profile → Delete account. If the server detects an unknown data dependency or Apple revocation cannot complete, it keeps local data and shows a retryable error.
- **Safety/content issue:** Skip or reveal the stop, then use “something off with this stop?” after it is revealed. For urgent danger, contact local emergency services; DayQuest is not an emergency service.
